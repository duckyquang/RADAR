How to interpret this score:
USABLE (0–29%) — Adequate demographic reporting. Race, sex, age, and geographic data are available in most cited studies. The evidence base can support diversity analysis. Proceed with subgroup comparisons and representativeness assessment.
QUESTIONABLE (30–59%) — Significant gaps in demographic reporting. Key variables (especially race/ethnicity or sex) are missing from many studies. Exercise caution — any diversity findings may be biased by incompleteness.
NOT USABLE (60–100%) — Critical demographic data is absent from the majority of cited studies. Reliable conclusions about participant diversity cannot be drawn. The guideline's evidence base needs more inclusive reporting before analysis is meaningful.
